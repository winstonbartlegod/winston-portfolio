# How to use this template

Duplicate this `.mdx` file, rename it to match your new slug, and then update:

- `title`
- `date`
- `excerpt`
- `tags`
- `image`
- Every section below

<Callout type="tip">
Put new blog images inside `public/images/blog/` and reference them with paths like `/images/blog/your-image-name.jpg`.
</Callout>

## Opening hook

Start with 1 to 3 short paragraphs that explain the topic, why it matters, and what the reader will learn.

This is where you can set the tone. Keep it personal, analytical, or practical depending on the kind of post you want to publish.

## Recommended article structure

1. Introduce the topic clearly
2. Explain the main problem or question
3. Add evidence, examples, or visuals
4. Share your interpretation
5. End with a concise conclusion
